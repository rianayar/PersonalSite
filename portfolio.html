My Portfolio

Keep scrolling to take a look at some of the projects that I have worked on over the past few years.

Hope for the Homeless

Check out thewebsitewe made as part of our 8th grade IB project to raise awareness of the homelessness situation in Tampa, Florida. Our group was able to learn more about the homeless population with extensive research and become aware of the impact local charities and nonprofits have on them.


FIRST Like A Girl

I coordinated the creation of a set of interview videos for our FIRST Robotics Team to support the #FIRSTLikeAGirl movement that are uploaded toYouTube.This movement supports gender equality in a STEM robotics club. We also made buttons and boards, and helped spread word through social media posts on our Team'sInstagram page.


Homeless Supply Drive

I spent a weekfundraisingto deliver non-perishable groceries, fresh produce, and sanitary products for homeless shelters across Tampa. These items were used in soup kitchens, homeless shelters, and homeless resident programs. I coordinated with Homeless Helping Homeless, Metropolitan Ministries, and Salvation Army of Tampa to figure out what products they need and the quantity of each. I was able to help provide for some homeless people during the Covid-19 pandemic.


Virtual Volunteering with Assisted Living Facilities

A group of friends and I have been setting up weekly zoom meetings with the residents of local Tampa senior homes in order to perform piano pieces for them and engage in conversations with them. This project started during the Covid-19 pandemic and will hopefully continue on past it.
